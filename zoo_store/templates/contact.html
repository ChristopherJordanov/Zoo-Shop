Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

What are your shipping policies?

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50. Orders typically ship within 1-2 business days and arrive within 3-5 business days. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout.

Do you offer returns or exchanges?

Yes, we accept returns within 30 days of purchase. Items must be unused and in original packaging. For pet food and treats, we offer a satisfaction guarantee - if your pet doesn't love it, we'll refund your purchase.

Do you have a loyalty program?

Yes! Our PurrfectPoints program rewards you for every purchase. Earn 1 point for every dollar spent, and redeem points for discounts on future purchases. Sign up for an account to start earning today.

Do you offer pet adoption services?

While we don't directly offer pet adoption, we partner with local animal shelters for adoption events. Check our events calendar or sign up for our newsletter to stay informed about upcoming adoption opportunities.
